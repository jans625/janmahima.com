Travel Agency Advert

Travel First — Turkey Destination Advertisement

Dynamic digital advertisement combining graphics, storytelling, and compelling visuals to promote travel and experiences in Turkey

Created a visually engaging promotional advertisement for Travel First, a travel agency highlighting Turkey as the featured destination. The project fused cinematic visuals, motion design, and narrative storytelling to inspire potential travellers and showcase the destination's unique appeal.

Key Features & Contributions

  • Conceptualised and produced the full advertisement from initial idea to final output.
  • Developed the creative direction, including storyline, visual mood, and key messaging to promote Turkey's culture, landmarks, and travel experiences.
  • Combined motion graphics, smooth transitions, and dynamic visuals to create an immersive and persuasive digital advertisement.
  • Designed and animated sequences that effectively communicate the excitement and beauty of travelling to Turkey.
  • Ensured the final piece was optimised for digital platforms, such as social media, website, and online campaigns.

Applications/Software/Technologies Used

  • Adobe Aero
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Fonts
  • Adobe Fresco

Outcome

Delivered a captivating advertisement that effectively promotes Travel First's services and the destination of Turkey. Demonstrated the ability to create emotionally engaging digital content that drives interest in travel experiences, and showcased skills in using modern tools to produce professional promotional media.
