Project

Brand Poster

"Coco Chanel" Brand Poster Design

A luxury fashion poster exploring timeless elegance, iconic branding, and sophisticated visual storytelling inspired by the legendary Coco Chanel

Created a high-end brand poster for Coco Chanel that captures the essence of timeless luxury, minimalist elegance, and the iconic spirit of the fashion house. The design blends historical reverence with contemporary aesthetics to produce a visually striking and sophisticated promotional piece.

Key Features & Contributions

  • Designed the full brand poster from concept to final execution, drawing inspiration from Coco Chanel's signature style, heritage, and philosophy.
  • Focused on luxury aesthetics through refined typography, elegant composition, classic color palette (black, white, gold), and timeless imagery.
  • Carefully crafted typographic hierarchy and layout to achieve visual balance and sophistication.
  • Developed multiple design variations while maintaining strong brand consistency and emotional impact.

Applications/Software/Technologies Used

  • Adobe Fresco
  • Adobe Illustrator

Outcome

Successfully translated the iconic Coco Chanel legacy into a modern, elegant poster design. Demonstrated strong understanding of luxury branding principles and ability to create visually compelling promotional materials. Created a portfolio piece that showcases both creative interpretation and technical proficiency in professional design tools.
