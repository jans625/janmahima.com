Interactive Documentary — Queensland Rail Museum

Immersive digital experience showcasing the history and heritage of the Queensland Rail Museum through interactive storytelling and production design

Worked as Production Designer and Interactive Designer on an engaging interactive documentary project focused on the Queensland Rail Museum. The project combined rich historical content with interactive elements to create an educational and captivating experience for visitors and online audiences.

Key Features & Contributions

  • Led production design, crafted the overall visual style, and contributed to environment aesthetics and thematic consistency across the documentary.
  • Designed and implemented interactive experiences that allowed users to explore museum artefacts, historical timelines, and rail heritage stories in a nonlinear and engaging way.
  • Collaborated closely with the creative and content team members to translate physical museum exhibits into compelling digital interactions.
  • Created user-friendly navigation, visual assets, and immersive interfaces that enhanced storytelling and user engagement.
  • Ensured the design maintained historical accuracy while delivering a modern, accessible digital experience.

Applications/Software/Technologies Used

  • Luma AI
  • Maya (Autodesk)
  • Luma Fly Through

Outcome

Successfully transformed the rich history of the Queensland Rail Museum into an accessible and interactive digital format. Created an educational tool that increases public engagement with Australian rail heritage, and demonstrated the ability to bridge traditional production design with modern interactive technologies.
