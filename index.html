About Me

Designing with authenticity, heart, and intent.

Welcome to my professional portfolio. Here you'll find a comprehensive overview of my work, professional experience, skills, and the passion that drives my practice in digital media.

My name is Janice Singh, though I'm often called Jan. I am currently in my third year at the University of Southern Queensland (UniSQ), pursuing a Bachelor of Design and Interactive Technology with a major in Digital Media and a minor in Film. Alongside my design studies, I have gained valuable experience in programming and business.

I see every project as a chance to forge genuine connections, spark inspiration, and breathe life into characters and concepts through digital media. My professional development includes targeted workshops in digital design, animation, 3D modelling, interactive documentary, film, and business process management. Together, these experiences have shaped my artistic practice, built my confidence and discipline, and helped me cultivate a distinctive creative voice.

This site presents an intimate window into my portfolio, showcasing my work, training, and the evolving creative journey I am nurturing day by day. My aspiration is to grow steadily, learn deeply, and bring authenticity and heart to projects of true significance.
